Introduction - Why are we here?

There are many ways that our clients can benefit from our technical expertise in Data at Telstra Purple. Generally, these fall into one of four core services that we provide:

  1. Good Data Architecture
    • The data is organised in a way that is manageable in production, easy for analytics professionals to use, and trusted as a source of truth within the business.
       
  2. Data Analytics & Insights
    • Cleaning and manipulating data to enable the calculation of business metrics, to show how a business is performing (and/or performed in the past). Visualisation comes into play here; results are usually presented in the form of a live dashboard, or a weekly/monthly report. Typically, the data sources will be structured.
       
  3. Data Science & Machine Learning
    • Manipulating the data in a way that answers a complex question (usually a business problem) and tells a deeper story about what is happening. Typically this includes predictions of what will likely happen in the future based upon historical data, and sometimes this will include machine learning algorithms. Good Visualisation is critical here, because the concepts are often difficult to explain without graphics/interactivity. Data sources will be a combination of structured and unstructured.
       
  4. IoT
    • Sometimes the data needed to solve a particular business problem doesn’t yet exist. We can help a customer by understanding what data is required (usually a design component here), then IoT devices can be installed to collect the required data and feed into the existing Data Architecture. The Data Analytics / Data Science work can be completed once enough data has been collected for a particular problem.

Using data well can be the difference between gaining or losing competitive advantage for our clients.
